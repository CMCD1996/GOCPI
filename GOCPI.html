ABOUT THE PROJECT

Model

The model adopts The Integrated Markel EFORM System (TIMES) Model to model the entire energy system for a given geography

Purpose

Communicate the urgency of addressing climate change while informing carbon pricing policy and sustainable investment strategies

Output

Geography specific services demand profiles and energy system recommendations

System

The model is written in GAMS with supporting functionality provided by Python, HTML, CSS and JavaScript

Experience

Connor is a Part IV Engineering Science Student. He has experience building mathematical and optimisation models. Connor has worked in several industries. These include Industrials, Professional Services, and Energy.
